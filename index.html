Bowker Family Meal Plan

Week of 22 February 2026 · All meals are gluten-free

Sunday
Herb-Crusted Chicken Breast with Roasted Sweet Potatoes & Steamed Broccoli
Chicken breast (lean) · 35 min cook
Prep chicken marinade Saturday. Batch-roast extra sweet potatoes for Tuesday.
Monday
Teriyaki Salmon with Jasmine Rice & Edamame Salad
Salmon fillet · 20 min cook
Use GF tamari instead of soy sauce. Quick 20-min meal.
Tuesday
Turkey Taco Bowls — Rice, Black Beans, Corn, Avocado, Salsa
Ground turkey (lean) · 25 min
Use leftover rice from Monday. Corn tortillas on the side (GF).
Wednesday
Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta (GF penne) with Spinach & Cherry Tomatoes
Shrimp · 20 min
Use GF pasta (Barilla or similar). Prep garlic butter sauce ahead.
Thursday
Slow Cooker Chicken Tikka Masala with Basmati Rice & Cucumber Raita
Chicken thigh (skinless) · Slow cooker
Set slow cooker in the morning. Use GF-certified spice paste.
Friday
Grilled Lean Steak with Baked Potatoes & Caesar Salad
Sirloin steak (lean cut) · 30 min
Trim visible fat. Use GF croutons or skip. Yoghurt-based Caesar dressing.
Saturday
Homemade Margherita Pizza on GF Bases with Side Salad
Mozzarella + optional chicken · Fun family cook
Use store-bought GF pizza bases. Let kids top their own!

20 minCook a large batch of jasmine rice (for Mon + Tue)
10 minMake teriyaki sauce: GF tamari, honey, garlic, ginger, cornflour
10 minPrep tikka masala marinade for Thursday chicken
10 minWash & prep salad veg — store in damp towel
